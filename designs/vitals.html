Smart helmet system — IoT & machine learning

Real-time concussion detection.

A smart football helmet that catches dangerous head impacts the instant they happen — sensing the hit, scoring its force, and alerting coaches in milliseconds, before a second impact can land.

1000Hz
Sample rate
<50ms

Concussive threshold
From guesswork on the sideline to a measurement at the point of impact.

Today, concussion calls rely on a kid reporting that something feels wrong — or a coach noticing symptoms after the damage is done. Axon closes that gap.

Built by a founder who sustained two Grade 2 concussions across a varsity football career, the system turns every head impact into data: force, rotation, and risk — recorded the instant it occurs. The mission is simple. Make football safer for all.

The architecture
01
Adafruit BNO085
A 9-DOF inertial measurement unit seated in the shell, detecting sudden acceleration and rotational change.
02
Particle Boron
An onboard microcontroller that reads and scores the sensor data at the edge of every hit.
03
Firebase Firestore
A NoSQL cloud database ingesting flexible impact records in real time.
04
FlutterFlow App
Coaches monitor player status live; athletes review their own post-activity statistics.

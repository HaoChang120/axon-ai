The hidden cost of impacts

Most concussions go undetected, misdiagnosed, or ignored.

Head impacts in football too often slip past coaches and trainers — and the damage compounds. Repeated undiagnosed hits are linked to CTE, memory impairment, depression, and early-onset dementia. The problem starts long before the pros.

360° sensor suite

A full sensor array, built into the shell.

Four pressure sensors map where every hit lands, while a gyroscope and accelerometer capture how hard and how fast the head moved — together feeding the detection model in real time.

SENSE
4 pressure sensors
Front, both sides, and rear — pinpointing impact location across the helmet.
MEASURE
Gyroscope + accelerometer
Captures linear force and rotational motion — the two drivers of brain injury.
PROCESS
On-board AI
A Particle microcontroller scores every impact the instant it happens.
ALERT
Cloud + mobile app
Data streams to the cloud; coaches get an instant alert on their phone.

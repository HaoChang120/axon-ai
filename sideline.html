The system

How Axon Works

Sensors in the shell
A BNO085 9-DOF IMU tracks linear + angular acceleration at 100 Hz. Four FSR pressure pads read impact force front, sides, and rear.
Particle Boron
The pads feed ADS1115 ADCs; everything lands on a Particle Boron over I²C. It scores each hit and publishes over LTE — no phone tether needed.
The cloud
Impacts stream to the Particle Cloud in milliseconds. This app subscribes to the live event feed — anywhere with a signal.
Your pocket
Coaches and players get a risk score and an instant alert the moment a dangerous hit lands — before a second one can.
The science
Axon scores risk with the Rowson–Duma combined probability model, which weighs linear and rotational acceleration together rather than a single g cutoff. Concussive hits typically reach ~95–100 g and ~4500–7000 rad/s². Your personal threshold shifts with neck strength, head mass, and hydration.
